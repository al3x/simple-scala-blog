C4 Wrapup

I can’t recommend C4 enough. It was a great conference in a great location at a great price, and a fantastic argument for keeping such events small and focused. It felt like a classroom, or rather like the sort of classroom I wish I’d had access to during my eduction. The long Q&A sessions and open dialog amongst speakers and attendees was supremely valuable. Hell, even the food was good. It’s hard to ask for much more.



I’ll be back next year without a doubt. Hats off to Wolf for putting it all together.
