It’s nice to know I’m not the only one feeling the way I do about
today’s hip-hop,
and it’s nice to know that it’s not simply an “outsider’s” perspective, if
you want to play the race game. Pierre Bennu’s <a
href=”http://www.exittheapple.com/applesauce/01/fuckhiphop.html”>”Fuck
Hip-Hop” rant communicates the desperate longing of real hip-hop fan,
and a DJ at that, for a reorientaation of values in the hip-hop
community. Read it.
