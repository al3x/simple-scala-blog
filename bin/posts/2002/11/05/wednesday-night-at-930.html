Wednesday Night at the 9:30

---
-

One of my first loves in the realm of electronica was Ninjatune, a largely downtempo record label without peer. Their “Funkungfusion” compilation, which I picked up in a little record shop in Pasadena, changed my musical world. With that in mind, I’m totally jazzed to see mad Brazilian glitch-loungehead Amon Tobin rock the 9:30 club this Wednesday with DJ Food (actually a multi-DJ endevour given a face by two Englishmen) and Bonobo (who I’ve never heard, but has Ninjatune to rest his laurels on). A full report will be offered.
