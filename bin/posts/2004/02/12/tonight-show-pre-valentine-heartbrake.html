Tonight’s Show: Pre-Valentine’s Heartbrake Songs

What a way to start a new semester of radio shows.



Normally I’d be doing shows both Tuesday and Thursday, but this week is a little funny. Tuesdays I reserve for genre explorations and more “themed” shows, and Thursdays I’m just playing whatever the hell I want. I feel like playing songs for anyone nursing a broken heart this Valentine’s Day, so that’s what I’m gonna do. You can tune in from 6-7PM EST on WMBC, whose MP3 streams should work more than not.
