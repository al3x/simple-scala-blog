Alternative Mac BitTorrent Clients

---
-

Sarwat Khan has whipped up a more full-featured expansion of the BitTorrent 3.3 code for Mac OS X, keeping the same name but piling on the features. The application feels like it could use some touch-ups for Panther, however.



Recommended to me was Azureus, a Java BitTorrent client with even more bells and whistles but, in my view, a long way to go on the GUI and stability. Still, it’s better than the generic OS X client.



Options, options.
