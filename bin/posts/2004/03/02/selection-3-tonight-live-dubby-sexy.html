I\’ve spent many an hour I didn\’t really have putting together this mix in Ableton Live, the technical details of which I\’ve praised and bemoaned on this blog over the past weeks. The first is more of an issue than the latter, as it leaves me “mixing blind,” but I\’m confident enough in my track order that I believe the transitions will be clean.

If you\’re a fan of the genre then I need say no more, but if you\’re not, you will be after this mix.
