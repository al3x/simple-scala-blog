Apple Sexiness Roundup

---
-

Steve Jobs has been busy at Macworld San Fransisco. Announced today:
both 12” and (!!!) 17” PowerBook G4s, with onboard
Bluetooth; Airport Extreme, Apple’s name for the higher-speed,
backwards-compatible 802.11g technology that pushes the existing 802.11b wireless
up to 54Mbps and tacks on better security; a whipass-fast new web browser
called Safari; a slick new
PowerPoint competitor called Keynote,
and even their own distribution of the X11 *nixland graphics layer. Crazy busy Apple.
