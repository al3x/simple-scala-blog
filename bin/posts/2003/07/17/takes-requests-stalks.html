Tony Pierce has a vanity domain, and on it a blog. The other day he had a long, amusing post about Friendster girls (a recent topic of interest to me), which concluded in the sentence “[A]nd one day my children will ask me why i married their mother and i will say [“]boredom and defeat.[“] [A]nd then i will ask him not to talk to me for the rest of the evening if its ok with them” (punctuation and capitalization mine, non-agreeing pronouns his). So I left, in all good humor, a comment on that post requesting, “Please do not have children.” In what is apparently his style, or ‘steez,’ he used my comment as inspiration for another thoroughly entertaining self-reflecting little blog nugget. And then AIM’d me to say so. And has apparently checked out the IP from which I posted.



Nicholas Chase I’m now quite comfortable with. This Pierce character, on the other hand… ;-)
