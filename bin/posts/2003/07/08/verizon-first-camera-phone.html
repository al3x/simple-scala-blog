Verizon’s First Camera Phone

---
-

Verizon finally has a camera phone. It’s $300 retail; that is, without the 2-year contract extension for existing Verizon customers like yours truly. That’s a lot more than the ”$150 after $50 rebate” price that they’ll be quoting everywhere, and seems like a pretty penny for a .4 megapixel resolution camera, no matter how good the phone around it is. I’ve ditched my previous digicam from lack of use, so having a camera with me would be nice. But I’m not sure if this is the way…
