Reason magazine is presently running a short comic by Peter Bagge that muses on the ineffectiveness and embarrassments of the American antiwar movement. He’s a Libertarian of the school that makes me almost identify with that platform, and his criticisms are both apt and biting. Quote of the day (referring to the Isreali/Palestinian conflict): “I [...] resent being forced to subsidize a staging ground for Armageddon, but does that mean I have to ‘root’ for the Palestinians? Can’t I loathe both sides equally? Where’s the ‘they can all go to hell’ booth? I want to volunteer!” I think I’ve said as much, many times over. Right on topic, the CSM has an article questioning the effectiveness of protests. The pro-protesters resounding chorus? “It worked for Vietnam…” cough
