TouchStream for PowerBook!

---
-

Holy cats! The geniuses at FingerWorks have made a drop-in TouchStream keyboard for Apple’s PowerBook G4! It’s efficient keylessness to go! If I had one of those aluminum beauties I’d be.. wishing it wasn’t so expensive. For now I remain quite happy with my TouchStream ST, though I gather they’ve now collapsed that line into TouchStream LP, which can be both placed on a frame like the ST or folded up and taken with you. Clever kids.
