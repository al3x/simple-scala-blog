Go go gadget Lazyweb! iTunes 4 can now display album art alongside songs and albums, but you have to put it in by hand. So, Lazywebbers, is there some way to automate the process of seeking out album art on the Web for all the albums in an iTunes library and then applying it? AppleScript-able?
