Little-Known Most Perfect IDM Album

---
- "

“Triskaideka” by Syndrone is my hands-down favorite quirky techno (or IDM, if you must) album. Yes, that’s right: above Autechre, Boards of Canada, all that lot. They may have style and sheen, but Syndrone bleeds emotion through his/her/its mess of glitch, distant pads, 8-bit melodies, and bass burbles. If you’re dry-eyed by the end of “Intrn|rrr” either you don’t get electronic music or someone turned off your emotion chip. There’s just something that aches from behind what would otherwise be a fairly formulaic combination of sounds. Sometimes it’s sad, sometimes beautiful, and often both.



Find it where I did, on eMusic. I didn’t realize they describe it as “aching” on their page too until I went to find that link, but there you go. Not just me.
